ABOUT ME

All about me and my hobbies

My name is Alosh Mathew, and I am a passionate 2D animator from Kerala. I am currently pursuing my studies in animation and graphic design to strengthen my creative and technical skills in visual storytelling and character animation. I have a strong interest in creating expressive animations, character designs, and illustrated scenes that bring ideas and emotions to life. My passion for animation motivates me to explore different art styles, motion techniques, and creative storytelling methods to improve my craft. Living in Kerala inspires my creativity through its rich culture, vibrant colors, and artistic traditions, which often influence my visual style and design approach. I enjoy developing characters, sketching concepts, and creating animated content that connects with audiences in a meaningful and engaging way. Alongside my studies, I continuously practice and work on personal and freelance projects to gain experience and refine my animation skills. My goal is to grow as a professional 2D animator and contribute to the animation industry by creating unique, creative, and impactful animated experiences that combine strong storytelling with appealing visuals.
